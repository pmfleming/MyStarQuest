Princess Experience Prototype

A focused interactive preview of the mobile reward flow.

Interaction Details

This prototype showcases the finalized scrolling experience for the app, focusing on visual cleanliness and native-feeling interactions.

  • Hidden Scrollbar: The native browser scrollbar is completely hidden to maintain immersion.
  • Drag to Scroll: Users can click (or tap) and drag the cards directly to move the list up and down.
  • Gradient Fade: A soft white/pink gradient sits at the bottom of the list, providing a subtle visual cue that more content exists below.
  • Tactile Feedback: Cards slightly press down while dragging and lift up on hover.

Test it out: Click and drag anywhere on the cards to scroll.
