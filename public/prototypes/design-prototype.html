🔬 UI/UX Research: Ages 4-6

🖐️ Motor Skills

  • Min touch target: 72×72px (19mm)
  • Element spacing: ≥20px
  • Use tap gestures only (no swipe/pinch)
  • Large, forgiving hit areas

🧠 Cognitive Load

  • Icons PRIMARY, text secondary
  • Max 3-4 nav items visible
  • Flat hierarchy (no nested menus)
  • Prominent "Home" button always

🎨 Color Psychology

  • Saturated colors engage attention
  • Avoid neon (causes eye strain)
  • Green = positive/go, Red = stop
  • High contrast for readability

✨ Feedback

  • Response time: <100ms
  • Exaggerated animations for success
  • Buttons should "press" visibly
  • Audio + visual combined
