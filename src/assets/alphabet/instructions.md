Canvas:

- Square image (same dimensions as previous images)
- Pure white background
- Minimize empty space at the top and bottom — objects should fill the vertical space as much as possible without being cropped
- Keep a small, consistent margin on all sides

Content:

- Exactly 3 items: [ITEM1], [ITEM2], [ITEM3]
- Center item slightly larger
- Even spacing, horizontally aligned
- Scale objects so they occupy most of the canvas height

Style:

- Rounded, adorable proportions
- Big glossy eyes (for animals)
- Soft shading and highlights
- Bright, saturated colors
- Clean soft outlines

Lighting:

- Gentle top-left lighting
- Subtle depth shading

Effects:

- Add 2–4 small golden sparkle stars around the subjects

Grounding:

- Add soft grey shadows directly beneath each object

Rules:

- Do not crop any objects
- Avoid excessive whitespace, especially vertically
